# 🌍 Explora

> Discover cities through experiences, not just places.

Explora is an AI-powered city exploration platform that helps people discover hidden gems, local food spots, historical landmarks, photography locations, tourist attractions, and unique local experiences.

The idea started from a simple problem:

When visiting a new city, people usually jump between Google Maps, travel blogs, Instagram reels, YouTube videos, and review platforms to figure out what to do. Even after spending hours researching, creating a good itinerary is still difficult.

Explora aims to solve this by combining discovery, maps, and AI-powered planning into a single platform.

---

## Why I'm Building Explora

Most map applications are great at navigation.

Most review platforms are great at ratings.

Most travel websites are great at showing attractions.

But none of them truly answer:

> "I have one day in this city. What should I do based on my interests?"

Explora is my attempt to build a smarter way to explore cities.

Instead of only showing locations, the platform helps users create meaningful experiences and personalized exploration routes.

---

## What Explora Will Do

Users will be able to:

* Discover hidden gems
* Find local food spots
* Explore historical landmarks
* Discover photography locations
* Find unique local experiences
* Generate AI-powered itineraries
* Save favorite places
* Explore cities through an interactive map

---

## MVP Features

### 🗺️ Interactive Map Discovery

Browse places directly on a map instead of scrolling through endless lists.

### 🍜 Food Discovery

Find famous local food spots and authentic local experiences.

### ✨ Hidden Gems

Discover places that don't usually appear in traditional travel recommendations.

### 📸 Photography Spots

Explore scenic viewpoints, street photography locations, and unique visual experiences.

### 🏛️ Historical Locations

Learn about the history and culture of a city through curated locations.

### 🤖 AI Itinerary Generator

Generate personalized routes based on:

* City
* Available time
* Interests
* Travel preferences

Example:

* Food + Photography + History
* Half-day trip
* Walking route

Explora generates a complete exploration plan automatically.
